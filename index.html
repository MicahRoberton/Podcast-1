Welcome to our Podcast!

Episode content is formulated from both hosts' backgrounds. Micah's humor and his avid book reading come together to point out intriguing ideas. Duncan brings a dialed in wealth of knowledge of history and insight that ties the broadcast together. As a team we provide an informative and funny approach to historical and current concepts!

We release all new episodes each week! Check back frequently to our ever evolving site and community! Thank You!
